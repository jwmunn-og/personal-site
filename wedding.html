Details

Planning a wedding is no light task and every designer knows the hardest client to please is yourself. Put the two together and you have one fun yet stressful recipe! Overall it was an amazing opportunity to explore & create in the handmade/printed worlds I rarely get to venture.

Aside from the normal design & code, I got to intimately know every curve of our script typeface Helinda Rook Std through doing calligraphy on the invitations and seating cards. I even got my hands dirty with some chalk lettering and ink illustrations. Thanks to our photographer and my wife’s impeccable style we even made a stint around the wedding blogoshpere.

Along the way I got to work with some incredible people all around the triangle: Digital Printing by Metro Productions, Letterpress by Shed Letterpress, and photography by Julia Wade.
