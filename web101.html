Details

Web101 is a bi-weekly course over 6 months that teaches the principles of web design. Throughout the branding, organizing, and launch I have been able to work with a fantastic team of people. Web development can be intimidating for the uninitiated so we wanted the branding to feel friendly and approachable. The site serves as a basic directory for any links the students in the class need to access on a regular basis. I will also be speaking on HTML emails for the course January so creating one for the launch of registration seemed only natural.
